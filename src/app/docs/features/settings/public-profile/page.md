The **Public Profile** page lets you control what clients see on your public booking page. It includes a live preview that updates as you make changes.

![Public Profile page with edit form on the left and live preview on the right](/images/settings/05-public-profile.png)

## Editing your profile

The left side of the page contains your profile form with these fields:

- **First Name** and **Last Name** -- Your display name on the public page
- **Practice** -- Your practice or business name
- **Specialization** -- Your coaching focus area (e.g., "Financial Coaching", "Executive Leadership")
- **Bio** -- A short description of your coaching style and experience
- **Profile Photo** -- Upload a photo or use the auto-generated avatar
- **Timezone** -- Your local timezone for scheduling
- **Public Page URL** -- A customizable slug (lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only)

Click **Save Profile** to apply your changes.

## Your public page URL

Your booking page is available at a unique URL based on your slug. The URL card shows your full public page address with two actions:

- **Copy link** -- Copy the URL to your clipboard for sharing
- **Open page** -- Preview your public page in a new tab

Share this link with clients so they can view your profile and book sessions.

## Live preview

The right side of the page shows a real-time preview of how your public page looks to clients. It includes:

- Your profile photo and name
- Specialization and bio
- Available session types with duration, location, and deposit information

The preview updates automatically as you edit the form fields.

## Custom branding

Below the preview, the **Custom Branding** section lets you personalize your public page and client emails with your own logo and colors.

### Enabling branding

Toggle the **Active** switch to turn custom branding on or off. When active, your logo appears on your public booking page and in invoice emails sent to clients.

### Uploading a logo

1. Click **Upload logo** (or **Replace logo** if one is already set)
2. Select a PNG, JPG, WebP, or SVG file (max 5MB)
3. A preview appears -- click **Save** to apply
4. To remove the logo, click **Remove** next to the current logo

### Brand colors

Set two colors for your brand:

- **Primary** -- Used for the hero banner on your public page and key accent elements
- **Accent** -- Used for secondary highlights

Click the color picker to choose custom values, or click **Reset to defaults** to return to CoachBase's default palette.

{% callout type="note" %}
Custom branding is available on the Pro plan and above. On the Solo plan, a link to upgrade is shown instead of the branding controls.
{% /callout %}

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use my own domain for the booking page?**
Not currently. Your public page is hosted at your CoachBase URL with your custom slug.

**What image formats work for the profile photo?**
CoachBase accepts standard image formats. If you do not upload a photo, an auto-generated gradient avatar is displayed using your initials.

**Does the logo appear on invoices?**
Yes. When custom branding is active, your logo appears at the top of invoice detail pages and in invoice emails sent to clients.
