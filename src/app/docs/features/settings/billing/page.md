The **Billing** tab shows your current subscription status and lets you manage your plan, payment method, and view past charges.

![Billing settings showing subscription status and plan options](/images/settings/04-billing.png)

## Choosing a plan

If you do not have an active subscription, the billing page shows a prompt to choose a plan. Click **Choose a plan** to view available tiers and start a free 14-day trial (if eligible) or subscribe directly.

## Managing your subscription

Once subscribed, the billing page displays:

- **Current Plan** -- Your plan name, renewal date, and status (Active or Trialing)
- **Change plan** -- Switch between plans or billing intervals (monthly/annual)
- **Payment method** -- The card on file with expiration date

### Changing your plan

Click **Change plan** to view and compare available plans. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between monthly and annual billing. When switching to annual billing, the page shows your estimated savings.

If you have a scheduled plan change, it appears with an arrow showing the transition from your current plan to the upcoming plan and the effective date.

### Updating your payment method

Click **Update** next to your payment method to change the card on file. A modal opens where you can enter new card details.

### Cancelling your subscription

1. Click **Cancel subscription**
2. A confirmation dialog shows when your access ends
3. Click **Yes, cancel** to confirm

After cancelling, you retain access until the end of your current billing period. You can reactivate at any time before that date by clicking **Reactivate**.

## Billing history

Below the subscription card, a billing history table lists your past charges with:

- **Date** of the charge
- **Description** (plan name and invoice number)
- **Amount** (with refund details if applicable)
- **Status** (Paid, Refunded, or Partial Refund)
- **View invoice** link to the Stripe-hosted receipt

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I switch from monthly to annual billing?**
Yes. Click **Change plan** and select the annual option. You save money compared to paying monthly, and the switch takes effect at your next billing cycle.

**What happens when my trial ends?**
Your card on file is charged automatically. If you cancel before the trial ends, you are not charged.

**Will I lose data if I downgrade?**
No. Your data is preserved when changing plans. However, features exclusive to higher tiers become unavailable until you upgrade again.
