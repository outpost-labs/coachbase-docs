The **Referrals** page lets you earn subscription credits by inviting other coaches to CoachBase. When a referred coach subscribes to a paid plan, you receive a $20 credit toward your own subscription.

![Referrals page showing referral link, credit info, and stats cards](/images/referrals/01-overview.png)

## Your referral link

At the top of the page, you will find your unique referral link. Click **Copy link** to copy it to your clipboard and share it with other coaches via email, social media, or messaging.

When someone signs up using your link:

- They get their **first month free** on monthly plans, or the equivalent discount on annual plans
- You earn a **$20 credit** once they subscribe to a paid plan

## Tracking referrals

Three stat cards show your referral performance at a glance:

- **Total referrals** -- the number of coaches who signed up using your link
- **Qualifying referrals** -- referrals where the coach subscribed to a paid plan
- **Total credits earned** -- the dollar value of credits applied to your account

## Referral history

If you have referrals, a history table appears below the stats showing each referral with:

- **Referral** -- the referred coach's name or email
- **Referred on** -- the date they signed up
- **Status** -- **Signed up** (registered but no subscription), **Trailing** (on a trial or has payment info), or **Subscribed** (active paid plan)
- **Credit** -- $20.00 for qualifying referrals, or a dash if not yet qualified

## How credits work

{% callout type="note" %}Referral credits are applied toward your subscription cost only. They cannot be used to cover payment processing fees.{% /callout %}

Credits are automatically applied to your next billing cycle. You can continue earning credits with no limit on the number of referrals.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**When do I receive my credit?**
Your $20 credit is applied once the referred coach subscribes to a paid plan (not during their free trial).

**Is there a limit to how many coaches I can refer?**
No. You can refer as many coaches as you like and earn $20 for each one who subscribes.

**Can I see who clicked my link but did not sign up?**
No. Only coaches who complete registration appear in your referral history.
