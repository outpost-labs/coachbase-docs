Workflow templates give you a head start by providing pre-configured workflows for common coaching scenarios. Choose a template, customize it if needed, and save.

![Workflow template library with five pre-built templates](/images/workflows/03-templates.png)

## Browsing templates

1. Go to **Workflows** and click **Use Template**.
2. Browse the available templates.
3. Click **Use Template** on the one you want.

CoachBase opens the new workflow form with the template's name, description, trigger event, and steps pre-filled.

![New workflow form pre-filled from the Welcome new client template](/images/workflows/04-new-from-template.png)

## Available templates

| Template | Trigger | What it does |
|----------|---------|--------------|
| **Welcome new client** | Client created | Sends a welcome email and creates an onboarding task |
| **Follow up after session** | Session completed | Waits one day, then sends a follow-up email |
| **Nurture inactive clients** | Client status changed (to nurture) | Creates a check-in task and sends a coach notification |
| **Invoice overdue reminder** | Invoice overdue | Sends a coach notification and logs a system message |
| **Celebrate goal achieved** | Goal achieved | Sends a congratulations email and notifies the coach |

## Customizing a template

After clicking **Use Template**, the workflow form opens with everything pre-filled. You can change any field before saving:

- Edit the **name** or **description** to match your style.
- Change the **trigger event** if you want a different trigger.
- Modify, reorder, add, or remove **steps** to fit your needs.
- Check **Enable this workflow** if you want it active immediately.

When you are satisfied, click **Create Workflow** to save.

{% callout type="note" %}
Templates create a new, independent workflow. Changing or deleting a workflow created from a template has no effect on the original template.
{% /callout %}

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I create my own templates?**
Custom templates are not currently supported. You can duplicate an existing workflow by creating a new one and configuring it with the same settings.

**Will templates change over time?**
CoachBase may add new templates in the future. Existing workflows you created from templates are not affected by template updates.

**Do templates count toward my workflow limit?**
Yes. A workflow created from a template counts the same as any other workflow toward your plan's limit.
