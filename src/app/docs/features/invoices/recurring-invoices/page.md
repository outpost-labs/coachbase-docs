Recurring invoices let you automate billing for clients on a regular schedule. CoachBase generates and optionally sends invoices automatically based on the frequency you define.

![Recurring invoices tab showing schedules with client, amount, frequency, next date, and status](/images/invoices/08-recurring-tab.png)

## Viewing recurring invoices

Switch to the recurring view by selecting **Type: Recurring** from the filter dropdown on the Invoices page. The recurring table shows:

- **Client** name
- **Amount** per invoice
- **Frequency** (e.g., Every month, Every 12 weeks)
- **Next Invoice** date
- **Status** (Active, Paused, Completed, or Cancelled)

Click any row to view the recurring invoice details. Hover to reveal view and edit action icons.

## Recurring invoice statuses

- **Active** -- The schedule is running and will generate invoices on the next date
- **Paused** -- The schedule is temporarily stopped. No invoices will be generated until resumed.
- **Completed** -- The schedule has finished (reached its end date or max invoice count)
- **Cancelled** -- The schedule was permanently stopped

### Filtering by status

Use the **Status** dropdown to filter recurring invoices. Options include All, Active, Paused, Completed, and Cancelled.

## Setting up a recurring invoice

To create a recurring invoice schedule, open the **New Invoice** modal and check **Make this recurring**. Configure:

- **Frequency** -- How often invoices are generated (e.g., every 1 month, every 2 weeks)
- **Start Date** -- When the first invoice should be generated
- **End Date** (optional) -- When the schedule should stop
- **Max Invoices** (optional) -- Maximum number of invoices to generate
- **Due date offset** -- How many days after generation each invoice is due (same day, 7, 14, 30, or 60 days)
- **Auto-send** -- Whether to automatically email each generated invoice

{% callout type="note" %}
If neither an end date nor a max invoice count is set, the recurring schedule runs indefinitely until you pause or cancel it.
{% /callout %}

## Managing a recurring schedule

From the recurring invoice detail page, you can:

- **Edit** the schedule settings (amount, frequency, end conditions)
- **Pause** an active schedule to temporarily stop generation
- **Resume** a paused schedule
- **Cancel** a schedule to permanently stop it

Individual invoices generated by the schedule appear in the one-time invoices tab and can be managed independently (sent, marked paid, voided, etc.).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change the amount on a recurring invoice?**
Yes. Editing the recurring invoice amount applies to future generated invoices. Previously generated invoices are not affected.

**What happens when I pause a recurring invoice?**
No new invoices are generated while paused. The next invoice date does not advance. When you resume, generation continues from where it left off.

**Can I delete a recurring invoice?**
You cannot delete a recurring invoice, but you can cancel it. Cancelling stops all future invoice generation. Previously generated invoices remain in your records.
