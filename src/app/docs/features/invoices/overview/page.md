The **Invoices** page gives you a complete view of every invoice you have created. From here you can filter by status, switch between one-time and recurring invoices, and quickly open any invoice for details.

![Invoice list showing all invoices with number, client, amount, status, and due date columns](/images/invoices/01-invoice-list.png)

## Browsing your invoices

The invoice table displays each invoice's number, client name, amount, status, and due date. Click any row to open the invoice detail page. Hover over a row to reveal quick-action icons for viewing and editing.

Invoices are sorted by creation date, with the most recent at the top. Overdue due dates appear in red for easy identification.

## Filtering by status

Use the **Status** dropdown above the table to filter invoices by their current status. Available filters for one-time invoices:

- **All** -- Show every invoice
- **Draft** -- Invoices not yet sent to the client
- **Sent** -- Invoices delivered to the client
- **Paid** -- Invoices that have been paid
- **Overdue** -- Sent invoices past their due date
- **Cancelled** -- Invoices that were cancelled
- **Refunded** -- Invoices where a refund was issued
- **Void** -- Invoices that were voided

![Status filter dropdown showing all available status options](/images/invoices/02-status-filter.png)

When a filter is active, the pill appears highlighted. Select **All** to clear the filter.

## Switching between one-time and recurring

Use the **Type** dropdown to toggle between **One-time** and **Recurring** invoice views. The recurring view shows a different set of columns including frequency, next invoice date, and recurring status.

## Settings menu

Click the gear icon next to the filters to access:

- **Manage Packages** -- Go to your packages list to create or edit coaching packages
- **Payments** -- Open the payments settings page to connect Stripe

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How are invoice numbers assigned?**
CoachBase automatically assigns sequential invoice numbers (INV-0001, INV-0002, etc.) when you create an invoice. You cannot change the number after creation.

**Can I search for a specific invoice?**
Use the status filter to narrow down the list. You can also click directly into any invoice from a client's detail page under their invoices tab.

**What does the overdue status mean?**
An invoice is marked overdue when it has been sent to the client and its due date has passed without payment.
