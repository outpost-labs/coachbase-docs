The **Analytics** page gives you a bird's-eye view of your coaching practice. It combines revenue metrics, session activity, and client health indicators into one dashboard so you can spot trends and take action.

![Analytics dashboard showing revenue cards, monthly chart, coaching activity, and client health](/images/analytics/01-overview.png)

{% callout type="note" %}
Analytics is available on the Pro plan and above. You also need the **View Analytics** permission enabled for your role.
{% /callout %}

## Filtering by period

Use the **Period** dropdown in the top-right corner to change the reporting window. Available options:

- **This Month** -- current calendar month (default)
- **Last 90 Days** -- rolling 90-day window
- **This Year** -- January 1 through today
- **All Time** -- your entire account history

All cards and charts on the page update to reflect the selected period.

## My Data vs. Account view

If your plan supports account-level reporting, two toggle pills appear below the page title:

- **My Data** -- shows metrics for your sessions, clients, and invoices only
- **Account** -- aggregates data across all coaches on the account

The Account view also includes an additional **Revenue by Coach** breakdown panel.

## Revenue

The Revenue section includes three summary cards:

- **Total Collected** -- sum of all paid invoice amounts in the selected period
- **Outstanding** -- total unpaid invoice balance
- **Avg Invoice Value** -- average amount across paid invoices

Below the cards, two breakdown panels show **Revenue by Client** and **Revenue by Package**, each with a horizontal bar chart and dollar amounts. These help you identify your highest-value clients and most popular packages.

### Monthly Revenue chart

A 12-month bar chart displays your monthly revenue trend. Each bar shows the collected amount for that month, making it easy to spot seasonal patterns or growth.

## Coaching Activity

Four metric cards track your session and client performance:

- **Sessions Completed** -- number of sessions marked complete, with a trend indicator comparing to the previous period
- **No-Show Rate** -- percentage of scheduled sessions where the client did not attend
- **Active Clients** -- clients with at least one session in the period, plus counts of new and churned clients
- **Lead Conversion** -- percentage of leads that converted to active clients

### Session Utilization

A progress bar shows how many sessions you completed relative to the estimated available slots from your availability schedule. This helps you understand how fully booked you are.

## Client Health

The Client Health section highlights engagement trends:

- **Avg Sessions per Client** -- average number of completed sessions per active client over the last 90 days
- **No Session in 30+ Days** -- lists active clients who have not had a session recently, so you can follow up

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I export my analytics data?**
Not currently. Analytics are viewable on the dashboard only.

**Why does the Account view show different numbers than My Data?**
Account view aggregates across all coaches on your account. If you have team members, their sessions and invoices are included.

**What counts as a "churned" client?**
A client is considered churned if they were active in the previous period but had no sessions in the current period.
