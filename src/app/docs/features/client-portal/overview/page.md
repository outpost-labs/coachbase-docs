The Client Portal is a private area where your coaching clients can log in to stay connected between sessions. Clients get their own dashboard with upcoming sessions, active goals, tasks, shared files, and direct messaging -- all branded to match your practice.

![Client portal dashboard showing welcome message and navigation](/images/client-portal/02-dashboard.png)

## What clients can do

Once invited to the portal, your clients can:

- **View sessions** -- See upcoming and past sessions, read session recaps, and add prep notes before their next call.
- **Track goals** -- Review active goals you've set for them and see which ones they've achieved.
- **Complete tasks** -- Check off action items you've assigned between sessions.
- **Download files** -- Access worksheets, templates, and other files you've shared with them.
- **Message you** -- Send and receive messages in a threaded conversation view.

## How clients log in

Clients sign in with a magic link -- no password required. They enter their email address, receive a secure link, and click it to access the portal. Links expire after 30 minutes and can only be used once.

## What you control

You decide exactly what each client sees. Portal visibility can be configured at the account level (defaults for all clients) and overridden per client. Sections you can show or hide include sessions, goals, tasks, files, messages, and the activity timeline.

{% callout type="note" %}
The Client Portal is available on the Pro and Practice plans. You can enable it from your account settings.
{% /callout %}

## Navigation

The portal includes a top navigation bar on desktop with links to each section: **Dashboard**, **Sessions**, **Goals**, **Files**, and **Messages**. On mobile, a fixed bottom tab bar provides the same navigation with icons for easy tapping.

Your brand logo and colors are applied automatically, giving clients a cohesive experience that feels like an extension of your practice.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Do clients need a CoachBase account?** No. Clients log in with a magic link sent to their email. They never need to create an account or set a password.
- **Can I control what each client sees?** Yes. You can enable or disable portal sections per account or per individual client.
- **Is client activity logged?** Yes. Every portal action -- logins, page views, task completions, messages -- is logged to an audit trail for your records.
