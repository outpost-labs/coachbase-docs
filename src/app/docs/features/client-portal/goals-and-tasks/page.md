The Goals page helps clients see the bigger picture of their coaching journey -- what they're working toward and what they've already achieved.

![Goals page with Active and Achieved tabs](/images/client-portal/05-goals.png)

## Viewing goals

Goals are organized into two tabs:

- **Active** -- Goals currently in progress, ordered by when they were created. Each goal card shows the title and a brief description.
- **Achieved** -- Goals that have been marked as complete. These show an "Achieved" badge so clients can see their progress over time.

## Completing tasks

Tasks are action items you assign to clients between sessions -- homework, exercises, follow-ups, or anything they need to do before your next meeting.

Clients can complete tasks in two places:

1. **Dashboard** -- Open tasks appear as a checklist. Clicking the checkbox marks the task complete and removes it from the list.
2. **Goals page** -- Tasks associated with a goal can be completed inline.

When a client completes a task, the completion is logged with a timestamp. Tasks cannot be "uncompleted" by the client -- this prevents accidental changes.

{% callout type="note" %}
Task completion is only available when the tasks section is set to "visible and completable" in your portal settings. If set to "visible only," clients can see their tasks but cannot check them off.
{% /callout %}

## How goals and tasks connect

Goals give direction. Tasks give action. Together, they create a structured coaching experience:

- You set goals during sessions to define what the client is working toward.
- You assign tasks between sessions to keep momentum going.
- Clients complete tasks and track their progress toward each goal.

## Empty state

When no goals have been set yet, clients see: "No active goals yet. Your coach will set goals as your coaching progresses." This sets the expectation that goals will come as the relationship develops.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Can clients create their own goals or tasks?** No. Goals and tasks are created by the coach. Clients can view and complete tasks but cannot add new ones.
- **What happens when a goal is achieved?** It moves from the Active tab to the Achieved tab with a badge. It remains visible for the client to look back on.
- **Can I hide goals or tasks from a specific client?** Yes. Portal visibility settings let you hide the goals and tasks sections per account or per individual client.
