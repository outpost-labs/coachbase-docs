The Sessions page gives clients a clear view of their coaching schedule -- upcoming sessions they need to prepare for and past sessions they can review.

![Sessions page with Upcoming, Past, and Timeline tabs](/images/client-portal/03-sessions-list.png)

## Viewing sessions

Sessions are organized into three tabs:

- **Upcoming** -- Future scheduled sessions, ordered by date. Each card shows the session date, time, duration, and location type (video call, in-person, etc.).
- **Past** -- Completed, cancelled, and no-show sessions. Completed sessions with a recap display a "Recap available" badge.
- **Timeline** -- A chronological view of all session activity in a vertical timeline format.

Click any session to open the detail view.

## Session details

The session detail page shows the full session information:

- **Date and time** -- When the session is or was scheduled.
- **Duration** -- How long the session lasts.
- **Location** -- Where the session takes place (video call link, phone, in-person address).

### Session recaps

After a session is completed, you can write a recap summarizing key takeaways, action items, and next steps. Clients see this recap on the session detail page, formatted for easy reading.

Recaps help clients retain what was discussed and give them a reference point between sessions.

### Prep notes

Before an upcoming session, clients can write prep notes -- topics they want to discuss, questions they have, or progress they want to share. The prep notes area appears on the session detail page for any future session.

{% callout type="note" %}
Prep notes can only be edited before the session. Once a session is completed or has passed, the notes become read-only to prevent stale edits.
{% /callout %}

## Empty state

When no sessions are scheduled, clients see the message: "No upcoming sessions scheduled. Your coach will schedule your next session." This reassures them that they're in the right place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Can clients schedule their own sessions from the portal?** Not directly from the portal. Clients book sessions through your public booking page, which is separate from the portal.
- **Do clients see cancelled sessions?** Yes, cancelled sessions appear in the Past tab so clients have a complete history.
- **Can I control whether clients see the Sessions section?** Yes. You can hide the sessions section entirely from portal visibility settings.
