The portal dashboard is the first thing clients see after logging in. It gives them a quick overview of their coaching journey -- upcoming sessions, active goals, open tasks, recent files, and unread messages.

![Client portal dashboard with welcome message](/images/client-portal/02-dashboard.png)

## Dashboard sections

The dashboard adapts to show only what is relevant. Sections appear based on your visibility settings and whether there is content to display.

### Join banner

When a session is about to start (within 15 minutes) or is currently in progress, a prominent banner appears at the top of the dashboard with a **Join Now** button. This links directly to the video call, making it easy for clients to connect without hunting for a meeting link.

### Session prep

If a session is coming up within 24 hours, the dashboard shows a prep notes area where clients can jot down topics they want to discuss. Notes are saved and visible to you before the session begins.

### Active goals

The first three active goals appear as a bullet list with their titles and a truncated description. A **View all** link takes clients to the full goals page.

### Action items

Open tasks you've assigned appear as a checklist. Clients can mark tasks complete directly from the dashboard -- the item disappears with a smooth transition. Due dates are shown next to each task. Up to 10 tasks are displayed.

### Recent files

The last five files you've shared with the client are listed with their name and the date they were added. An orange dot appears next to files added since the client's last visit. A **View all** link opens the full file library.

### Unread messages

If the client has unread messages from you, the **Messages** navigation link shows a count badge so they know to check in.

## Empty state

When a client first logs in and there are no sessions, goals, or tasks yet, a welcome message explains the portal's purpose: "This is your space to prepare for sessions, track your goals, and stay connected with your coach."

## Frequently Asked Questions

- **Can I customize what shows on the dashboard?** The dashboard automatically shows sections based on your portal visibility settings. Hide a section (like tasks or files) and it won't appear on the dashboard either.
- **Do clients see other clients' information?** No. Each client only sees their own sessions, goals, tasks, files, and messages. Data is strictly isolated per client.
- **What does the "new" dot mean on files?** The orange dot indicates content that was added since the client's last portal visit, helping them spot what's new.
