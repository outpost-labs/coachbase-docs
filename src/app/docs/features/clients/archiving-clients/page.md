Keep your client list clean by archiving inactive clients, and keep client details up to date as your coaching relationships evolve.

## Editing a client

1. From the client list, hover over a row and click the edit icon. Or from a client's detail page, click the edit icon in the top right.
2. Update any fields -- name, company, job title, email, phone, status, or description.
3. Click **Update Client**.

![Edit client form with pre-filled fields](/images/clients/15-edit-client-form.png)

## Archiving a client

Archiving removes a client from your default client list without deleting their data. All sessions, invoices, notes, and goals are preserved.

1. Open the client's detail page.
2. Click the archive icon in the top right action bar.
3. The client moves to the **Archived** filter view.

{% callout type="warning" %}
Archiving is not the same as deleting. All client history is fully preserved and accessible.
{% /callout %}

### Viewing archived clients

From the **Clients** page, open the **Stage** filter and select **Archived** to see all archived clients.

### Unarchiving a client

1. Filter to **Stage: Archived** on the client list.
2. Open the archived client's detail page.
3. Click the restore icon in the top right action bar.

The client returns to their previous stage and reappears in the main client list.

## Custom fields

{% callout %}
Custom fields are available on the Pro plan.
{% /callout %}

Custom fields let you track additional information on every client record -- referral source, industry, coaching program, or anything specific to your practice.

### Defining custom fields

1. From the **Clients** page, click the settings icon (gear) in the filter bar.
2. Select **Custom Fields**.
3. Add fields with one of four types:
   - **Text** -- Free-form text input
   - **Number** -- Numeric values
   - **Date** -- Date picker
   - **Select** -- Dropdown with predefined choices

Custom fields appear on the new client form, edit form, and the client detail header card.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I delete a client instead of archiving?**
Yes, but archiving is recommended. Deleting permanently removes the client and all associated data. Archiving preserves everything while keeping your active list clean.

**Do archived clients count toward any limits?**
No. Archived clients do not appear in your active client list or affect any plan limits.

**Can I bulk-archive multiple clients at once?**
Yes. Select multiple clients using the checkboxes on the client list, then click the archive icon in the bulk actions bar that appears.
