Pipeline stages help you track where each client is in your coaching relationship -- from initial lead through active engagement.

## Default stages

CoachBase organizes stages into three groups:

### Pipeline (pre-sale)

These stages track prospects before they become paying clients:

- **Lead** -- Initial contact or inquiry
- **Contacted** -- You have reached out
- **Consult Scheduled** -- A discovery or consultation call is booked
- **Consult Completed** -- The consultation has taken place
- **Proposal Sent** -- You have sent a coaching proposal or agreement
- **Decision Pending** -- Waiting for the prospect's decision

### Engaged (active)

These stages are for clients you are actively working with:

- **Client** -- Currently engaged in coaching
- **Nurture** -- Between engagements or in a lighter-touch phase
- **Alumni** -- Completed their coaching program

### Lost

- **Lost** -- The prospect or client chose not to proceed

## Changing a client's stage

You can update a client's stage in two ways:

1. **From the client list** -- Click the stage badge on any row to open the stage dropdown and select a new stage.

![Inline stage dropdown showing all available stages](/images/clients/10-inline-stage-dropdown.png)

2. **From the client detail page** -- Click **Edit** and change the **Status** field.

## Customizing stage labels

You can rename any stage to match your coaching practice terminology. For example, you might rename "Consult Scheduled" to "Discovery Call Booked."

1. From the **Clients** page, click the settings icon (gear) in the filter bar.
2. Select **Client Stages**.
3. Enter custom labels in the text fields next to each default stage.
4. Click **Save Labels**.

![Client Stages page showing customizable labels for each stage](/images/clients/09-client-stages-page.png)

Leave a field blank to use the default name. Your custom labels appear everywhere stages are shown -- in the client list, detail pages, and filter dropdowns.

{% callout type="note" %}Custom labels are account-wide. All team members will see the same stage names.{% /callout %}

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I add entirely new stages?**
Currently, CoachBase provides a fixed set of stages that you can rename. You cannot add or remove stages.

**What is the difference between Pipeline and Engaged stages?**
Pipeline stages are for prospects who haven't started coaching yet. Engaged stages are for active or past clients. The client list filter lets you quickly view one group or the other.

**Does changing a client's stage trigger any automation?**
Stage changes are tracked in the client's Activity timeline, but they do not trigger automatic emails or notifications.
