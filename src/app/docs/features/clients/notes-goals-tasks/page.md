Each client has dedicated tabs for notes, goals, and tasks. Use these to track session insights, set coaching objectives, and manage action items.

## Notes

Notes let you record observations, action items, and breakthroughs from your coaching sessions.

![Notes tab showing pinned and regular notes with categories](/images/clients/11-notes-tab.png)

### Adding a note

1. Go to a client's detail page and select the **Notes** tab.
2. Click **Add Note**.
3. Enter your note content, select a category (Action Item, Observation, Breakthrough, etc.), and save.

### Pinning notes

Pin important notes so they appear at the top of the list. Pinned notes are marked with a pin icon and stay visible regardless of how many newer notes you add.

### Editing and deleting

Click **Edit** on any note to update its content or category. Click **Delete** to remove it.

## Goals

Goals help you and your client track progress toward specific coaching objectives.

![Goals tab showing active and achieved goals with target dates](/images/clients/12-goals-tab.png)

### Creating a goal

1. Select the **Goals** tab on a client's detail page.
2. Click **Add Goal**.
3. Enter the goal description, category, and target date.

### Tracking progress

Each goal shows its status: **Active** or **Achieved**. When a client reaches a goal, click **Achieve** to mark it complete. Achieved goals appear with a strikethrough and a checkmark.

Goals also display their category (such as Business or Personal) and target date.

## Tasks

Tasks are actionable to-do items for you or your client.

![Tasks tab with Add Task button](/images/clients/13-tasks-tab.png)

### Managing tasks

1. Select the **Tasks** tab on a client's detail page.
2. Click **Add Task** to create a new item.
3. Mark tasks complete as they are finished.

{% callout type="note" %}
All notes, goals, and tasks appear in the client's Activity timeline, giving you a unified view of your coaching engagement.
{% /callout %}

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can clients see my notes?**
No. Notes are private to your coaching practice. Only you and team members with access to your account can see them.

**Is there a limit to how many goals I can create per client?**
No. Create as many goals as needed to support the client's coaching journey.

**Do completed tasks disappear?**
No. Completed tasks remain visible so you have a full record of what was accomplished.
